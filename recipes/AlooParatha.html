Aloo Paratha

Description:-

# Aloo Paratha is a stuffed Indian flatbread filled with a savory mixture of mashed potatoes, green chilies, and spices. It is a staple breakfast in North India, especially Punjab, and is best enjoyed with butter, curd, or pickle. Its crispy exterior and soft, flavorful filling make it a comforting and satisfying dish.

Ingredients:-

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 3 boiled and mashed potatoes
  • 1 chopped green chili
  • 1/2 tsp ajwain (carom seeds)
  • Chopped coriander leaves
  • Salt, red chili powder, garam masala
  • Ghee or butter for frying

Steps To Make the Item

  1. Knead soft dough with whole wheat flour and water; let rest for 20 minutes.
  2. Mix mashed potatoes with spices, chili, ajwain, and coriander.
  3. Roll out a dough ball, place filling, and seal it.
  4. Gently roll again into a flat circle.
  5. Cook on hot tawa with ghee until golden and crisp on both sides.

Serving Suggestions:-

Serve hot with yogurt, pickle, or butter.
