Masala Dosa

Description:-

# Masala Dosa is a beloved South Indian dish made from a fermented rice and urad dal batter, spread thin and cooked crisp on a griddle, then filled with a spiced mashed potato filling. It’s usually served with coconut chutney and sambar. Masala Dosa is not just a breakfast favorite but also a crowd-pleasing street food and restaurant specialty across India.

Ingredients:-

  • 2 cups dosa batter
  • 3 boiled and mashed potatoes
  • 1 sliced onion
  • 1 chopped green chili
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 8–10 curry leaves
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil or ghee

Steps To Make the Item:-

  1. Heat oil, add mustard seeds, curry leaves, and green chili.
  2. Add onions and sauté until translucent.
  3. Add turmeric, salt, and mashed potatoes; mix well and cook for 2–3 minutes.
  4. Heat tawa, spread dosa batter thinly, and cook till crisp.
  5. Add the potato filling, fold the dosa, and serve hot.

Serving Suggestions:-

Serve with coconut chutney and sambar for a complete meal.
