Paneer Butter Masala

Description:-

# Paneer Butter Masala is a luxurious vegetarian curry that combines soft cubes of paneer (Indian cottage cheese) in a creamy, mildly spiced tomato-based gravy enriched with butter and cream. This dish is a restaurant favorite, known for its silky texture, slightly sweet notes, and balanced spice. It pairs wonderfully with naan, roti, or jeera rice.

Ingredients:-

  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 pureed tomatoes
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp cream
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp sugar (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 tsp kasuri methi

Steps To Make the Item:-

  1. Heat butter, saut√© onion and ginger-garlic paste till golden.
  2. Add tomato puree, chili powder, and garam masala; cook until oil separates.
  3. Add paneer cubes and a little water; stir gently.
  4. Add cream, sugar (if using), and kasuri methi; mix and simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with naan or rice.

Serving Suggestions:-

Serve with naan, roti, or jeera rice, and a side of salad or raita.
