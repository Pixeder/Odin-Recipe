Biryani

Description:-

# Vegetable Biryani is a fragrant and colorful rice dish made with aromatic basmati rice, fresh vegetables, and a blend of traditional Indian spices. It is layered and slow-cooked (dum method) to infuse the rice with rich flavors. Often served with raita or salan, biryani is a celebratory dish enjoyed across India in many regional styles.

Ingredients:-

  • 1.5 cups basmati rice (soaked)
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (peas, carrot, beans, etc.)
  • 2 sliced onions
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp biryani masala
  • Whole spices (bay leaf, cinnamon, cardamom, cloves)
  • Mint and coriander leaves
  • 2 tbsp saffron milk
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil/ghee

Steps To Make the Item:-

  1. Cook rice partially with whole spices; set aside.
  2. In a pan, fry onions till golden. Add ginger-garlic paste and vegetables.
  3. Mix yogurt, biryani masala, salt, mint, and coriander; cook till veggies are tender.
  4. Layer rice and masala alternately in a heavy pan.
  5. Top with fried onions and saffron milk. Cover and cook on low heat (dum) for 15â€“20 minutes.

Serving Suggestions:-

Serve hot with raita, salad, or a side of curry.
