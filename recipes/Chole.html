Chole (Chickpea Curry)

Description:-

# Chole is a popular North Indian dish made from white chickpeas simmered in a rich and tangy onion-tomato gravy infused with aromatic spices. Often served with bhature (deep-fried bread), roti, or rice, this hearty dish is a staple in Punjabi households and is loved for its bold flavors and comforting texture. It’s perfect for festive occasions, family dinners, or even as a street food delicacy.

Ingredients:-

  • 2 cups boiled chickpeas
  • 1 finely chopped onion
  • 2 pureed tomatoes
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 green chili (slit)
  • 1.5 tsp chole masala
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)

Steps To Make the Item:-

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Stir in ginger-garlic paste and green chili; cook till raw smell goes.
  4. Add tomato puree and cook with turmeric, chili, and chole masala until oil separates.
  5. Add boiled chickpeas and some water; simmer for 10–15 mins.
  6. Garnish with chopped coriander and serve hot.

Serving Suggestions:-

Serve with bhature, puri, or rice. Accompany with onion salad and lemon wedges.
